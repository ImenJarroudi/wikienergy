Instructions

  1. Login to your Comed account.
  2. Click on the My Usage tab, (two tabs over from the house icon).
  3. From the left hand panel click Use My Energy Tools
  4. Now click on the large power button which says My Energy Tools
  5. This will take you to a new page.
  6. Click on the My Energy Use tab on the top of the page.
  7. At the bottom of the page there is a Green Button which says Download My Data. Click on this tab.
  8. In the popup which will appear after selecting the green button, click the Export all bill totals as an XML file.
  9. Complete this form, in which you will approve DSSG to access your smart meter data.
  10. The form will give you an option to upload your data.
